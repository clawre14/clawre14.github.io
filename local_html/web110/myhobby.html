My Hobby

Drawing

Hi My name is Cameron. As a kid, I used to Draw alot and I had dreams of being a professional artist. My drive to get better at drawing had diminished around the 8th grade when My aspirations and dreams shifted elsewhere. My interest in art was reignited a few months ago as I witnessed my 6 year old sister's passion for drawing. I'm currently just doing one sketch a day as I study from a book by Burne Hogarth, "Drawing The Human Head". I eventually want to get to a point to where I can confidently take on commissions and be 100% certain that I can consistently produce a high-quality artwork to clients. I still have a long way to go
