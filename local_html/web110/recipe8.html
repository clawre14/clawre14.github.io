Beef Stew Recipe

Ingredients

Measurement Ingredients
2 cups Beef Broth
1lb Beef Chunks
1/5 cups Chopped Carrots
1/4 cups Chopped Onions
2 Potatoes Chopped in eighths
1tbsp Salt
1tbsp Pepper
Instructions
Combine all items into a crock pot and place a cover on it. Allow the mixture to cook on lowest setting for 8 hours. Serve with rice.
